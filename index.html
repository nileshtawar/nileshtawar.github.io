About

My parents gifted me my first computer when I was a 13 year old kid. I had no idea about any of the Computer Science fundamentals up until I joined college with Computer Science as a subject. That's when I realised that logic and coding came naturally to me.

Later I pursued Computer Engineering and that is where I started realizing my true potential. (Yeah, it took me a while). I started attending events, workshops and hackathons. I wasn't the best one around but I never gave up. I started using and contributing to Open Source software and managed a Android Club in my graduation. By the way, check out my awesome work.
